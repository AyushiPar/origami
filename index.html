fun facts on camels!

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or dromedary camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

fun facts on chameleons!

  • There are around 160 species of chameleon.
  • Species of chameleon can be as small as 15 mm (0.59 in) or as large as 69 cm (27 in).
  • Chameleons are not deaf but they do not actually have ear openings.

fun facts on pigeons!

  • Pigeons have strong muscles used for flying. They can fly at the altitude of 6000 feet.
  • Pigeons are herbivores (plant-eaters). Their diet consists of seeds, fruit and various plants.
  • Pigeons have exceptional eyesight and ability to identify objects on a distance of 26 miles.

fun facts on teddy bears!

  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.
  • The teddy was named after President Theodore Roosevelt, after he refused to shoot a bear during a 1902 hunting trip.
  • A teddy has been into space! Magellan T Bear boarded Space Shuttle Discovery in 1995 as part of a school project.
